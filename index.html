About Brie

Brie has been practicing yoga for over 4 years. She has practiced Hatha and Vinyasa Style practices. She loves doing inversions, and any pose that challenges her. Her favorite type of yoga is hot yoga. Her hobbies outside of yoga include traveling, hiking, scuba diving, and adventure sports. Most importantly Brie enjoys sharing her passion for yoga with others.
